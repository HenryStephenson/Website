Brief Introduction

Hello! My name is Henry Stephenson; welcome to my website! I'm a 20-year-old Bioengineering student at McGill University with minors in Applied Artificial Intelligence and Finance (Desautels Management).

I have a keen interest in biological and biomedical research and entrepreneurship, and I enjoy studying linguistics, language (especially French, Chinese, and German), classical music, Roman history, architecture, and computer science/AI. I used to play soccer at a semi-professional level and have a love of sports.

I've worked in three laboratories during my undergraduate studies: the Dorval Lab of Biomaterials and Biodevices, the McGill iGEM Laboratory, and the Ostrowski Lab at U of T. More recently, I've also completed an internship at Nomic Bio on their Assay Engineering and R&D Team. Learn more about my research experiences below.
